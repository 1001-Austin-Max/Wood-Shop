My First Project - The Freshman Lamp

Max Austin - 2013

Introduction to the Shops

This project is the most important of any of my endeavors in the wood shop. Why? Because it was the first time I felt so capable and realized the abilities the Lick-Wilmerding wood shop granted me. As a freshman I wanted so badly for my lamp to be perfect but I realized the shops are all about speed bumps.

Design Process

As any freshman can attest, the design process for the lamp is... long. It was the most time I have spent designing a project. It started with the hundred design day,

a day where the freshmen draw a hundred different lamps to explore all different design styles and find what works for them

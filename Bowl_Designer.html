Design a Segmented Bowl with Max Austin's Bowl Designer

Making a segmented bowl is tricky, because it requires much more planning than other turning projects. Each segment of your bowl has to be perfect or the layers won't come out right. That's why the Segmented Bowl Designer is so helpful. By using this application you can quickly and easily plan out the dimensions of your bowl and generate a cutlist from your design
